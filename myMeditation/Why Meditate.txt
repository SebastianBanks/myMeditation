The practice of meditation has many benefits, just like how you exercise and diet to keep your body healthy. Meditation can be used to maintain a healthy mind. Practicing meditation has a long list of benefits that will not only be noticeable to you but also the people around you.

Meditation can:

- Reduce stress and anxiety
- Improve your ability to focus
- Stable your emotions
- Improve sleep
- Gain insight on yourself
- Develop love and compassion
